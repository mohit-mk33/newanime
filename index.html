The Scope of Animation

The world of animation is vast and brings you a variety of opportunities. The following are the must-know if you are planning your career:

Scope based on designation

Being an animator does not always mean bringing in the section that creates the motion. You can also plan to get into the field as a modeler, clean-up artist, layout artist, compositor, texture artist, rigging artist, lighting artist, image editor, and much more. The designation changes the pay scale and brings with it different roles to perform. Once you get into the field, you can rise and shine as per your interests.

Based on types

There are majorly five types of animation that you can pick for your career. The choice depends on your interest

Traditional animation

It is the type that involves you drawing every single frame and merge them for the motion. It is also known as cell animation. It is still in demand and includes drawing on paper or computers and tablets.

2-D animation

It is less strenuous work and involves moving a character through vectors and editing frames. It is a type that uses rigs and reduces the work of re-drawing the image. Therefore, if you do not want to rely on drawing, it is an option!

3-D animation

They are specific individuals who help to create BI-specific applications for firms. It usually includes the creation of SAP, chart makers, tables, and much more. They come in demand with the need and change and vary as the demand and evolving bases.

Motion Graphics

It is the stream of animation that does not just include characters. It involves the use of texts, graphics, shapes, and much more to add extra grace. It is the one that is common for promotions, explanatory videos, and logos.

Stop motion

If you wish to choose animation, it can be one of the best options. It is a good option as it involves using objects around. It is the career that uses changing the position of it until the entire frame gets complete.
